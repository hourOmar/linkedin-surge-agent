# Writing Style

## Purpose

This file defines how LinkedIn content should be written and formatted. The LinkedIn Surge Agent follows these guidelines to produce clear, engaging, and professional posts consistent with ENTROGX's communication standards.

## Source

Official Sources:

- ENTROGX Brand Voice Documentation
- LinkedIn content guidelines (if available)

---

# General Style

Content should be:

- Clear
- Professional
- Concise
- Educational
- Engaging
- Optimistic

---

# Sentence Style

- Use short, impactful sentences.
- Prefer active voice.
- Keep language easy to understand.
- Explain technical concepts simply.

---

# Paragraph Structure

- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use whitespace for readability.
- Avoid large blocks of text.

---

# Storytelling

When appropriate:

- Begin with a strong insight or observation.
- Introduce the challenge.
- Explain the solution or lesson.
- End with a meaningful takeaway.
- Support stories with real examples whenever possible.

---

# Writing Focus

Prioritize:

- Outcomes over features.
- Real-world impact.
- Practical value.
- Educational insights.
- Thought leadership.

---

# Evidence & Credibility

- Support claims with data whenever possible.
- Reference measurable outcomes, statistics, or results when available.
- Use real examples and case studies instead of vague claims.
- Avoid unsupported statements or exaggerated promises.

---

# LinkedIn Formatting

- Start with a strong opening hook.
- Use line breaks for readability.
- Keep posts easy to scan.
- Avoid unnecessary formatting.

---

# Calls to Action

When appropriate:

- Encourage discussion.
- Invite opinions.
- Inspire collaboration.
- Ask thoughtful questions.

Avoid aggressive sales-oriented CTAs.

---

# Language Guidelines

Use language that is:

- Positive
- Inclusive
- Professional
- Confident
- Authentic

Avoid:

- Clickbait
- Sensational language.
- Excessive jargon.
- Overly promotional wording.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before content is considered complete, ensure that it:

- Is easy to read.
- Uses short, impactful sentences.
- Uses active voice.
- Maintains a positive and optimistic tone.
- Supports claims with data or real examples whenever possible.
- Focuses on outcomes rather than features.
- Reflects the ENTROGX brand.
- Supports meaningful engagement.
- Maintains a professional B2B tone.
