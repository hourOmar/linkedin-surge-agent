# Example Posts

## Purpose

This folder contains real LinkedIn posts published by ENTROGX Ventures. These posts are used as reference examples for the LinkedIn Surge Agent to learn the company's writing style, tone, messaging, and content structure.

## Contents

- `example_posts.md` – Collection of real ENTROGX LinkedIn posts organized by content type.

## Guidelines

- Only include real posts published by ENTROGX.
- Do not generate or rewrite posts using AI.
- Keep the original wording whenever possible.
- Add new posts as they are published to keep the Knowledge Base up to date.

## Content Types

The examples may include:

- Partnership Announcements
- Event Participation
- Podcast Promotions
- Educational Content
- Community & Ecosystem Updates
- Founder Visibility
- Company Announcements
- Thought Leadership
